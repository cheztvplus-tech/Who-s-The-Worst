📋 How to Play

  1. The Instigator silently chooses one player and places their vote.
  2. The Instigator cannot vote for themselves.
  3. Other players vote by guessing who they believe the Instigator chose, not who they'd personally pick.
  4. Once everyone has voted, the Instigator reveals all votes.
  5. Players matching the Instigator's vote get 1 point.
  6. If nobody guesses correctly, the majority vote gets 1 point AND the Instigator also gets 1 point.
  7. Double Down: Use once per game (or every round if host allows). Correct = 2 points, Wrong = lose card (or -2 pts in harsh mode).
  8. Next round: New Instigator goes round-robin.
